TICKETS​ ​YOU​ ​CAN​ ​TRUST

Utilising​ ​Ethereum-based​ ​blockchain,​ ​Blocktix​ ​provides​ ​a​ ​simple​ ​and secure​ ​solution​ ​ to​ ​distribute​ ​counterfeit-proof​ ​event​ ​tickets​ ​as​ ​well​ ​as facilitate​ ​trustless​ ​ p2p​ ​transfers​ ​of​ ​ownership.​

The​ ​smart​ ​contracts​ ​are provably​ ​secure​ ​and​ ​undergo​ ​rigorous​ ​external​ ​security​ ​auditing.
